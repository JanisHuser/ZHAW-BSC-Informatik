## Matrizen

### Multiplikation

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
\color{red}a_{11}  & \color{red}a_{12}\\
a_{21}  & a_{22}\\
\end{pmatrix}
\cdot
\begin{pmatrix}
\color{red}b_{11}  & b_{12}\\
\color{red}b_{21}  & b_{22}\\
\end{pmatrix}

=

\begin{pmatrix}
a_{11} \cdot b_{11} +
a_{12} \cdot b_{21} &
a_{11} \cdot b_{21} +
a_{12} \cdot b_{22}\\

a_{21} \cdot b_{11} +
a_{22} \cdot b_{21} &
a_{21} \cdot b_{21} +
a_{22} \cdot b_{22}\\
\end{pmatrix}
$$
